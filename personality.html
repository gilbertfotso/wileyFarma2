Wiley Farma's Personality

Wiley Farma is a handsome and very kind young boy who is ready to help everyone. he's also an artist (he makes music with his pet). In B-land they move with flying cars, flying pets (or large walking pets), some others also have magic ways of transportation. (there's no gas in B-land and those other things that pollute the air).
